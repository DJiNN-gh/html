Communitary Music
Welcome to our music school. We are music lovers and our objective is to share with you the musical appreciation.
Our professors are well-trained in a wide range of musical instruments. There are classes of piano, violin, guitar, cello, trumpet and others.
Here, everyone help each other. We value the interaction between people. The assistence is very important for the learning process.
In group, anyone from any age participate with their instrument. We love group practices, because we believe that is the best way to practice and learn.

Visit us

Our classes happen at the Tuileries Garden, right next to the Rivoli Street behind the Tuileries station.

Our schedule

The classes are open during Saturdays and Sundays, between 08:00 and 14:30.
